About Me

My name is Binbin Zhang (张彬彬). I'm the creator of the open source project WeNet, which is a popular, production first and production ready end-to-end speech recognition toolkit. WeNet is now maintained by Mobvoi, NWPU, and wenet-e2e.
My research interests include speech, especially speech recognition.
