About Me

My name is Binbin Zhang (张彬彬). I was advised by Prof. Lei Xie at NWPU. my research interests include speech and corresponding applications, especially for speech recognition.

I'm the creator of the open source project WeNet, which is a popular, production first and production ready end-to-end speech recognition toolkit. WeNet is now maintained by Mobvoi, NWPU, and wenet-e2e.

I'm one of the designers and teachers of the open course "语音识别：从入门到精通"(Automatic Speech Recognition), I'm responsible for the GMM-HMM, DNN-HMM, discriminative training and LF-MMI parts of the course.

Please visit my Zhihu to follow my other work.
